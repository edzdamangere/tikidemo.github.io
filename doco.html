Shining a light on the stories that have shaped our nations, Untold Pacific History is a landmark documentary series that re-examines the pivotal moments of our past through a uniquely Pacific lens.

From the early arrivals of explorers to the complex dynamics of colonial influence and the resilient rise of sovereign identities, this series ventures deep into the archives to bring forth the narratives that have often been overlooked or misconstrued. It is an exploration of truth, memory, and the enduring spirit of our people.

Through rare archival footage, expert historical analysis, and the powerful oral traditions of our elders, Untold Pacific History provides a vital educational resource and a profound tribute to the legacy of those who walked these islands before us.
